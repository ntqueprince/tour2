Travel Guide

Step-by-step kaise jaaye (How to reach)

1

Delhi → Dehradun

Hindi: Kashmiri Gate ISBT se raat 11 baje govt bus pakdo. Subah 6 baje Dehradun pahuch jaoge.

English: Take a government bus (UPSRTC / Uttarakhand Roadways) from Kashmiri Gate ISBT at 11 PM. You'll reach Dehradun by 6 AM.

2

Where to take bus?

Hindi: Kashmiri Gate ISBT (Delhi Metro Yellow + Red line). Platform 2-3 par Dehradun ki bus milti hai.

English: Kashmiri Gate ISBT — accessible via Delhi Metro (Yellow & Red lines). Buses leave from platforms 2-3.

3

Dehradun → Mussoorie

Hindi: Dehradun ISBT se bahar nikalte hi shared taxi (Sumo/Innova) milti hai. ₹150 per person, 1.5 hour ka safar.

English: Right outside Dehradun ISBT, you'll find shared taxis (Sumo / Innova) — ₹150 per person, ~1.5 hr ride uphill.

Important Tips

Yeh galti mat karna! (Don't make these mistakes)

🎒

Light Luggage

Sirf zaruri saaman lo. Pahaadon mein chalna easy hoga.

🚖

Share Taxi

Private taxi mehngi (₹1500). Sharing mein bas ₹150!

☕

Avoid Fancy Cafes

Mall Road ke fancy cafes mein chai ₹150! Local dhaba try karo.

🌙

Book Night Bus

Raat ki bus se time + hotel ka ek din ka kharcha bachega.

🧥

Carry Warm Clothes

Pahaadon mein raat ko thand hoti hai, even in summer.

💵

Carry Cash

UPI sab jagah nahi chalti. ₹2000 cash zaroor rakho.
